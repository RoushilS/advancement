How percentages are calculated

Percentages show the historical advancement rate for teams in each category based on this season's events.

For example, if "2nd place award" shows "75%" with "15/20", it means 20 teams won a 2nd place award this season (at events matching your selected team size), and 15 of them finished with an advancement rank at or above the "Advancing" cutoff.

2-way table

The 2-way table shows advancement rates for combinations of two factors. Each cell shows the advancement rate for teams with that specific result on both factors.

Point system tab

The Point system tab re-ranks every event under point values you choose and recomputes who would advance, so you can compare your system's advancement rates against the official ones.

Striped cells

Cells with diagonal stripes indicate low sample size (4 or fewer teams). These percentages may be less reliable.
